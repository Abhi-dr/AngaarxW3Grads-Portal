1. Introduction

Welcome to The Angaar Batch ("we," "our," "us"). We are an EdTech platform dedicated to providing quality educational content, including free classes and workshops. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of our users. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to safeguarding it.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect a limited amount of information directly from you when you register for an account, class, or workshop:

  • Contact Information: Your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Parent/Guardian Information: For users under the age of 18, we require the email address of a parent or legal guardian for consent and communication purposes.
  • Technical Information: We may automatically collect non-personal information like your browser type or IP address to improve our website's functionality and security. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To register you for our free classes and workshops and to communicate important information about them.
  • To Communicate: To send you updates, confirmations, and information related to the services you have signed up for. We will not send you marketing emails without your explicit consent.
  • To Improve Our Platform: To understand how our services are used and to make technical improvements.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is not shared with any third parties for marketing purposes. We will only disclose your information if required by law or to protect the safety and security of our users and our platform.

5. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes using encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important. Our platform is open to users above the age of 5. For any user under the age of 18, we operate as follows:

Parental Consent is Mandatory: We require a parent or legal guardian's email address to obtain verifiable consent before collecting any personal information from a child. All communications regarding the child's participation will be sent to the parent or guardian.

  • We collect only the information that is reasonably necessary for a child to participate in our free educational activities.
  • We do not create public profiles for children or allow direct messaging between users on our platform.
  • Parents or guardians have the right to review the information we have collected from their child, request its deletion, and refuse to allow any further collection or use of the child's information.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and, if applicable, sending a notice to the email address on file.
