How to Find Your Certificate ID

Your certificate ID is usually found at the bottom of your certificate or on your Email. It follows the format: ANGAAR-XXXX###

  • Check your email confirmation
  • Look at the certificate PDF
  • Contact support if lost

Certificate Processing Time

After event completion, certificates typically take:

  • 1-3 days: For automated verification
  • 3-7 days: For manual review cases
  • 7-15 days: For complex or large events

Common Issues

  • Certificate not found: Double-check the ID format
  • Pending status: Wait for approval or contact support
  • Wrong details: Report to the support team
  • Technical issues: Try refreshing or different browser
